🧠 RESET Support Guide
🔴 Critical Incident Response
After tough calls
- Ensure scene is cleared and crew is safe
- Initiate informal check-in within first hour
- Normalize reactions ("what you're feeling is common")
- Avoid forcing discussion
- Offer follow-up within 24–72 hours
👥 Peer Check-In
One-on-one support
- Ask open-ended questions
- Listen more than you talk
- Avoid fixing or minimizing
- Watch for withdrawal or behavior changes
- Reinforce confidentiality
⚠️ When to Escalate
Know your limits
- Suicidal thoughts or statements
- Substance misuse concerns
- Persistent sleep issues or distress
- Significant behavioral changes
- Refer to professional resources immediately
🧠 Self Check (Responder)
Check yourself too
- How am I feeling after this call?
- Am I carrying this home?
- Do I need to talk to someone?
- Use RESET resources if needed
