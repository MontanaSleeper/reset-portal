🧠 RESET Debrief Guide
1️⃣ Opening the Debrief
- Set a calm tone
- "This is a safe space to talk"
- Participation is voluntary
- Keep it confidential
Keep it simple. Don’t over-explain.
2️⃣ What Happened (Facts)
- Walk through the call step-by-step
- Let crew describe what they saw/did
- Avoid judgment or correction
This grounds everyone in the same event.
3️⃣ Reactions
- "What stood out to you?"
- "How did that feel?"
- Normalize responses
- Allow silence
Don’t force people to talk.
4️⃣ Support & Coping
- Reinforce normal stress reactions
- Encourage talking with peers
- Offer RESET follow-up
This is support, not counseling.
5️⃣ Closing
- Summarize briefly
- Provide resources if needed
- Identify anyone needing follow-up
- Thank the group
End on a steady, supportive tone.
⚠️ Watch For (Escalation)
- Withdrawal or shutdown
- Anger or unusual behavior
- Statements of hopelessness
- Substance concerns
If concerned → Open Resources or Call RESET
